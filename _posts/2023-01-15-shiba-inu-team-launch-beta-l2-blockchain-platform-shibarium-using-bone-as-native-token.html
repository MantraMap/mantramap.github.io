--- layout: post title: SHIBA INU Team Launch Beta L2 Blockchain Platform Shibarium Using $Bone As Native Token tag: EthWorldNews --- Shiba Inu Team announced the launch of the beta L2 blockchain platform (Shibarium) that aims to lower gas fees on the Ethereum network. Transactions on Shibarium occur “off-chain” and are communicated back to the Ethereum blockchain, which allows for lower costs and faster processing times. Shibarium also provides a robust development framework for the community to build their vision into the blockchain, and has potential uses in gaming, social media, education, and supply chains.
