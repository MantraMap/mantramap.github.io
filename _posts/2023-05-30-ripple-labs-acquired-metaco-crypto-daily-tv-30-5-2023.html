Shiba Inu-themed Floki sees trading volume surge

Floki spiked over 10% and saw its highest trading volumes in over three weeks as traders bet on the tokens amid a China-focused push for its Valhalla metaverse game. Trading volumes for the tokens, which are fashioned after the Shiba Inu dog breed, jumped to over $60 million, up from last week’s $25 million average. 

Ripple Labs acquired Metaco

In the most publicized acquisition within the crypto world in 2023, blockchain payments company Ripple Labs acquired Metaco. Metaco is Swiss-based asset custody that focuses on trading and decentralized finance. The acquisition was crucial as it could help Ripple Labs cement its footprints in the EU, after paying $250 million to seal the deal.

Russia scraps plans for a national cryptocurrency exchange

The Russian government is no longer planning to launch a regulated national cryptocurrency exchange but rather wants to regulate multiple crypto trading platforms. After introducing a plan to create a national crypto exchange in November 2022, Russian lawmakers have abandoned the idea.
