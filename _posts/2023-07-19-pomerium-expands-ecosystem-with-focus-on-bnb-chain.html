Singapore, Singapore, July 19th, 2023, Chainwire

Pomerium recently announced the listing of Pome Survival NFT on Binance NFT. Pomerium is a Web3 gaming studio that is expanding its project ecosystem centered around the BNB Chain.

One of the key features of Pomerium, which has released two Web3 games on the BNB Chain since 2022, is its expansion of the ecosystem on the BNB Chain as a single chain, setting it apart from other Gamefi projects that are expanding their projects through multi-chain approaches.

In June, Pomerium was selected as one of the twelve projects for the Zero2Hero incubator program hosted on the BNB Chain. Following this activity, the sale of Pome Survival NFT: BNB Edition is scheduled on Binance NFT.

SY KIM, the representative of Pomerium, stated, "It is an honor to have joined the esteemed Zero2Hero program hosted on the BNB Chain. The listing on Binance NFT, coupled with our collaboration with Ret Games, the developer of Pome Survival, has opened up a great opportunity for us.”

Pome Survival is being developed by Ret Games, a prominent blockchain company in South Korea that recently signed a partnership agreement for blockchain industry cultivation with the city of Incheon. Pomerium, on the other hand, is responsible for publishing the Web3 Gamefi project.

The sale of Pome Survival NFT: BNB Edition will commence on July 26th on the Binance NFT homepage.

About Pomerium

Pomerium is a Web3 multi-game platform that focuses on mobile games. Its unique token economy is designed to create a sustainable mobile gaming ecosystem, and its platform enables gamers to play games on mobile devices instead of being limited to PC or web browsers. Pomerium plans to expand its services and influence in the GameFi market in the coming years.
