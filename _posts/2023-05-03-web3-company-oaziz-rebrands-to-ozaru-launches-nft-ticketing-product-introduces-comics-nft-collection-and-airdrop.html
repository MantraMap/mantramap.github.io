Dubai, UAE, - Oaziz, a web3 company in the event industry, is excited to announce a major rebranding, including a name change to Ozaru. Along with the rebranding, Ozaru is launching a revolutionary NFT ticketing product and an NFT collection to celebrate the product launch.

With this development, Ozaru is set to revolutionize the way events are managed, starting with a smart-contract based NFT-ticketing system. The NFT ticketing product is set to be launched on May 16th, 2023.

In addition to this, Ozaru has undergone a complete rebranding, changing its name from Oaziz to Ozaru. The company's new name and brand identity reflect renewed focus and commitment to innovation and creativity in the web3 space.

Alongside, Ozaru has launched an NFT collection based on their Ottomakan Stories comics. These NFTs represent a unique and exclusive piece of digital artwork that allows collectors to own a piece of the Ozaru universe. To celebrate the launch of the NFT collection, Ozaru plans to conduct an NFT airdrop. This airdrop will provide collectors with the opportunity to own these exclusive NFTs and participate in the growing community of blockchain enthusiasts.This is the company's second airdrop. During the first one, the team attracted more than 5000 participants, and more than 500 received their NFTs for completing tasks and actively participating in the project's life.Ozaru Founder & CEO, Addy Creeze, expressed his excitement about the company's new developments, stating:

"We are thrilled to launch our go-to-market NFT ticketing product. Our mission has always been to empower event organizers and creatives with innovative tools that help them succeed, and we believe that our latest developments will bring us one step closer to realizing this vision. This is just a start, a lot of new tools are coming."
