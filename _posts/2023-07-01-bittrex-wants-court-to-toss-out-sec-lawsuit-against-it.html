Crypto exchange Bittrex has taken a significant step in its legal battle with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) by filing a motion to dismiss the lawsuit brought against it. In the recent court filing, Bittrex contends that the SEC lacks the authority to regulate crypto assets as securities without specific authorization from Congress.
