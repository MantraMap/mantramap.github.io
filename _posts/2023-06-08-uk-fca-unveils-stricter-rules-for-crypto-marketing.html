--- layout: post title: UK FCA Unveils Stricter Rules For Crypto Marketing canonical_url: https://en.ethereumworldnews.com/uk-fca-unveils-stricter-rules-for-crypto/ tag: EthWorldNews --- The Financial Conduct Authority of UK has unveiled stricter rules for crypto marketing in the country. The new rules will prevent crypto firms from offering "refer a friend" schemes and will require new crypto investors to have a 24 cooling off period.
