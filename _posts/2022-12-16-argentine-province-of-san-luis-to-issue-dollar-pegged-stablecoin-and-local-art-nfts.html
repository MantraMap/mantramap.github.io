--- layout: post title: Argentine Province of San Luis to Issue Dollar-Pegged Stablecoin and Local Art NFTs tag: Bitcoin.com --- San Luis, a province of Argentina, has approved a bill allowing it to issue a blockchain-based, dollar-pegged stablecoin. The bill, which also approves the issuance of local artistic assets as NFTs (non-fungible tokens) seeks to enable the promotion of social development, and economic, cultural, and financial inclusion using blockchain.
