Additionally, another notable observation from van de Poppe is the current “lack of interest” in the cryptocurrency markets. This diminished interest, according to the analyst, is responsible for more pronounced price corrections compared to what’s usually seen during bull market cycles. Such market sentiment can significantly influence how cryptocurrencies, including XRP, perform and are perceived by potential investors.
