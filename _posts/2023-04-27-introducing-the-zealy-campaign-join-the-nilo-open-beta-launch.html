Discover the World of Nilo: Brush-to-Earn

Nilo is revolutionizing oral care by combining a Smart Toothbrush with a digital collectible companion. By using the Nilo Smart Toothbrush, you earn Brushing Experience Points (BXP) for your dynamic NFT avatar. These earned BXP entitle you to a portion of the weekly NLT distribution based on the global BXP earned from active brushing. More details on what makes the Nilo token economy unique will be available soon.

The Nilo Smart Toothbrush and the app also offer users a wide range of rewards, such as Nilo NLT tokens that can be used to level up your avatar, purchase digital items, and even save on real-world oral care expenses. With Nilo's 'Brush-to-Earn' model, brushing your teeth is an exciting earning opportunity encouraging users to maintain their oral health.
