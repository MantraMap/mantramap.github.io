With a market cap of $6.9 billion, MATIC ranks as the 12th-largest cryptocurrency. Is A Bullish Run On For MATIC? According to MATIC’s price movement, the token appears to be in an ascending channel recording higher highs and lows in the last month. If MATIC manages to break out of its current consolidation zone, it is expected to only trade as high as $0.77, which represents its next resistance level.
