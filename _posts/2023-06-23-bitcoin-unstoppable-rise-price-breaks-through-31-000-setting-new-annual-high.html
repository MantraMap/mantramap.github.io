As the chart above indicates, Bitcoin’s ADX (Average Directional Index) is currently peaking upwards, signifying the current trend’s strength. The ADX is a technical indicator commonly used to quantify the strength of a trend in the financial markets. The ADX calculation is based on a moving average of the price range expansion over a given period. It measures the degree to which a market is trending, and it provides traders with a way to identify the strength of a trend and determine whether it is worth trading. When the ADX rises, the trend gains strength, while a falling ADX indicates that the trend is losing momentum. A high ADX reading suggests a strong trend is in place, while a low ADX reading suggests that the market is in a range-bound or choppy state.
