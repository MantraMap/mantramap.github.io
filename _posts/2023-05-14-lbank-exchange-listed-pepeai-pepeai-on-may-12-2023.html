About PEPEAI Token

93.1% of the PEPEAI tokens were sent to the liquidity pool, LP tokens burnt, and contract was renounced. The remaining 6.9% of the supply was held in a multi-sig wallet only to be used as tokens for future centralized exchange listings, bridges, and liquidity pools.

Based on ERC-20, PEPEAI has a total supply of 420.69 trillion (i.e., 420,690,000,000,000) tokens. It was listed on LBank Exchange at 10:00 UTC on May 12, 2023, investors who are interested in PEPEAI can easily buy and sell it on LBank Exchange now. The listing of PEPEAI token on LBank Exchange will help it further expand its business and draw more attention in the market.
