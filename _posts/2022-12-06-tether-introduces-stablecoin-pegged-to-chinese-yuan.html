Tether, the company that created the world’s largest stablecoin, has announced the launch of an offshore Chinese Yuan-backed stablecoin. The new stablecoin, Tether Introduces CNHT, a Stablecoin Pegged to the Chinese Yuan, brings together two of the world’s largest economies and allows users to transact with the Chinese Yuan (CNH) in a secure, digital form.
