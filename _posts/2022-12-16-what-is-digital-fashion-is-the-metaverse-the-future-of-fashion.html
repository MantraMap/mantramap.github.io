What is Digital Fashion? Digital fashion refers to clothing, accessories, and other fashion items that are created and worn in a digital world (Metaverse), rather than in the physical world. Digital fashion can take many forms, including: Virtual clothing and accessories for avatars in the metaverse, such as Second Life or The Sims 3D models
