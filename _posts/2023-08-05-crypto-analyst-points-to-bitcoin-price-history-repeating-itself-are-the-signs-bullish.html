Thereafter, the token is expected to undergo a major re-correction finding support in the first week of November 2023.  Based on the reference price cycle, Bitcoin could then experience a significant price drop in the last month of 2023 but close the year in a strong consolidation zone. Source: Glassnode Beyond 2023, the BTC market could also be set for huge profits in 2024 and 2025 if Bitcoin is indeed repeating the 2013-2017 price cycle during which the market leader famously gained by over 1600%.
