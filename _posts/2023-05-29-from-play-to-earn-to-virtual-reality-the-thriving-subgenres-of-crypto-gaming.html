The crypto game market is still young, but it's expanding quickly. Several reasons contribute to its expansion, making it more appealing to players. The growing acceptance of blockchain technology is one such cause. Owing of its safety, openness, and decentralization, this technology is used in various sectors, including the gaming industry.

The rising need for interactive and immersive experiences is also contributing to the expansion of the crypto gaming industry. These days, players want games that feel more like the real world, and crypto games provide that. 

Another appealing feature of crypto gaming is the possibility of profiting from crypto. Participating in games, performing tasks, and winning tournaments all provide players with opportunities to earn cryptocurrency. The development of the crypto sector is stimulated by this monetary incentive to participate in crypto gaming.

While still in its infancy, the crypto gaming sector shows great promise as a future powerhouse in the gaming sector. More sophisticated crypto games will likely be created as the sector matures, with more options for players to earn cryptocurrency and engage in immersive and interactive experiences.

Play-to-Earn Games

The popularity of Play-to-Earn games has skyrocketed in the crypto gaming community because they allow players to earn crypto as they play. These games' economies are based on cryptographic tokens and are constructed using blockchain technology. As a result of the popularity of these games, more play-to-earn games have emerged, making this genre one of the most exciting in cryptocurrency gaming companies.

Virtual Reality Games

While the concept of virtual reality (VR) games is not new, incorporating blockchain technology has increased the sense of realism experienced by players. Blockchain technology makes a safer and more transparent method of exchanging virtual assets and monetizing gaming experiences possible. Players of VR games have access to a 3D digital environment in which they may create, play, and sell their assets. Notably, this subgenre of crypto gaming also shows great potential for development and expansion.

This article will focus on 2023's best crypto games and where they rank. We will investigate which games have the greatest room for expansion and innovation based on their existing offers and future ambitions. By examining these games, we seek to inform players about the state of the crypto gaming industry and direct them toward the games with the most potential.

My Lovely Planet

My Lovely Planet by Ubisoft Lab is a mobile game with a simple but powerful idea. It lets you have fun while making a difference in the real world. As a player, you take on the part of a Lovely Protector whose goal is to make a safe, green, and beautiful island. There are minigames in the game that let you earn seeds, tickets, and stars that you can use to plant trees and get new pieces of scenery, and build places. Every time a tree is planted in the game, a real tree is also put in the real world. This makes it an unforgettable moment.

My Lovely Planet has already made a big splash, with 8,000 regular players, 30,000 members, and a 4.7 review on the App Store and Play Store. On the other hand, the game's goal is to bring together 100 million players by 2030 to plant 1 billion trees, clean up 1 million tons of trash from the ocean, and protect 40,000 threatened species. My Lovely Planet got the Unity For Humanity award for making a difference and is planning a token sale to reach more people and add new features, including its token sale coming up in a few days.

Blocklords

Blocklords, a strategy game resembling Age of Empires and Crusader Kings, allows players to begin as characters from various social classes and accumulate a fortune by conducting conflicts and collecting taxes. The community-first approach of MetaKing Studios is revolutionizing the game development industry, allowing players to experience the game, provide feedback, and collaborate with developers to influence the game's direction.

Blocklords is well on its way to becoming a significant force in the gaming industry, having received a staggering $15 million in pilot stage funding from well-known game industry titans such as Square Enix, Krafton, Fun Plus, Makers Fund, BITKRAFT, Delphi Digital, Animoca Brands, and ShimaCapital, among others. The Community-Beta version of the game is presently available, enabling players to experience its distinctive features and gameplay mechanics. Notably, by entering the community and obtaining a free in-game hero, players are able to collaborate with others, expand their empires, and defeat their adversaries for free.

OVAL3

OVAL3 is an innovative concept that fuses sports, gaming, and technology to create a one-of-a-kind Rugby gaming experience. The game provides a realistic and exciting take on the sport of Rugby by letting players collect, play, and win actual digital cards. OVAL3 has licenses for 42 clubs, over 2,000 players, and three leagues in France and the United States, and it seems set to shake up the gaming sector. Additionally, OVAL3 has partnered with Web3 industry leaders ERA2140, Pyratz Labs, and Markchain to provide state-of-the-art technical and promotional assistance.

Numerous aspects of OVAL3 make it a desirable choice, including the fact that the world's finest Rugby player, Antoine Dupont, is both an ambassador and a stakeholder. There are already over 3,000 users in the game's marketplace, and weekly transactions total over $30,000. The game is slated for release shortly, and the company is launching a token sale in the next few days to create the Rugby ecosystem token, which combines sports, gaming, and finance freshly and innovatively.

Fungiball

Fungiball is a one-of-a-kind fantasy game developed on the Polygon blockchain by combining professional tennis with NFTs. In-game NFTs reflect the current market worth of real-world tennis players. Avatars may be customized by the user, given NFTs, and used in tournaments for prizes. In Fungiball, NFTs stand in for different tennis abilities, and their relative scarcity establishes their relative value.

In addition to providing a great gaming experience, Fungiball also includes a marketplace for NFT trading and a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that encourages participation from the community in policymaking. Players participating in tournaments and challenges can win Ethereum, NFTs, and other unique prizes. Rareness is used to classify the NFTs that stand for different tennis abilities. There is a well-defined plan in place for the future of Fungiball and a solid staff, partnerships, and licensing back it. The initial auction of 48 NFTs will occur during the French Open in early June, and the game's release is scheduled for the end of the year.
