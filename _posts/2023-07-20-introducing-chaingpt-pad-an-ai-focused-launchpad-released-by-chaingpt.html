ChainGPT Pad, the IDO platform that will be enabling the next generation of startups in the crypto, blockchain, and Web3 industry has just been released

ChainGPT, the provider of AI-powered Infrastructure for the digital economy has just launched the ChainGPT Pad; a platform that connects promising early-stage startups with the $CGPT community and enables a new era of initial DEX offerings.

Now projects of all calibers will have an environment to supercharge their social presence, bootstrap their communities, and bring their products to market with greater impact.

What is the ChainGPT Pad?

The ChainGPT Pad is an IDO platform and incubator for Web3 projects looking to bring their ideas to life and jumpstart their token ecosystems. As a means to uphold the standards of ChainGPT, the Pad conducts rigorous quality assurance tests and due diligence processes to vet incoming projects and is highly selective with who they allow to list.

The launchpad is coupled with the ChainGPT incubation program that provides a network of expert mentors (founders, venture capitalists, and CEOs of other successful projects) that provide support to all projects that meet a certain qualification criteria.

What are some of the Platform Features?

The ChainGPT Pad has been geared up with all of the leading innovations that contribute to successful, healthy, and effective IDO launches. Striking the perfect balance between opportunity and security the ChainGPT Pad has been carefully designed by a team of professionals with experience working on some of the industry's leading launchpads.

Two Round Structure 

Every IDO is given two rounds for the audience to participate; starting at the guaranteed allocations for round 1 (where the upper tiers of the $CGPT community members are given exclusive access). In order to participate in the guaranteed allocation, qualifying members must register their interests (info on registration here). Moving into round 2, the FCFC (first-come-first-serve) invites members of all tiers and partners. 

Staking & Point System

Tithed to the $CGPT token ecosystem, the Chain GPT Pad has a simple and effective structure for establishing tiers. One $CGPT token is equal to one Point, point multipliers are based on the staking pool durations. An in-depth guide to the Staking and Point logic systems can be found here.

Fair Tier System

A model of prioritization and inclusion based on a balanced point module that leverages staking the $CGPT token to signal commitment. Composed of four distinct grade categories, Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Diamond, the tier system provides a structure for the discerning user that looks for maximal benefits. To explore the benefits of each tier and understand the detailed scope of inclusion, please refer to the official post here.

Flexible Refund Policy

As a means to provide the highest possible grade of protection to the participants of ChainGPT Pad’s IDOs, a refund policy has been implemented that allows users up to 14 days of post-round considerations to finalize their commitments and lock in their allocations. More information about the refund policy can be found here.

For questions pertaining to how projects can register, how users can join, restrictions of countries, requirements to signal interest, and everything in between ChainGPT has provided a dedicated F.A.Q. for the Pad which can be accessed here.

Upcoming IDOs on ChainGPT Pad

 

Immediately upon launch, ChainGPT orchestrated a world-class lineup of three startups that have been going through the incubation program and have prepared to launch their IDOs in the months of July-August. With three projects already establishing their technical aptitude and social presence, here is who will be launching through the ChainGPT pad: DexCheck, TradeTomato, and Solidus AI Tech.

What is DexCheck:

DexCheck is an analytics platform augmenting the user experience with an AI-powered toolkit. From whale tracking and leaderboard monitoring to mirror trading and even arbitrage scanning, DexCheck is the future of on-chain intelligence.

What is Tradetomato:

Tradetomato is using artificial intelligence to fuel its decentralized trading platform and portfolio management ecosystem. Catering to a wide audience through modular trading algorithms, a social marketplace, unique wallet aggregators, and other elements from its stack of advanced tooling, Tradetomato is an end-to-end solution for the automation of on-chain finance.

What is Solidus AI Tech:

Providing AI infrastructure solutions for enterprises through its suite of SaaS, BaaS, AIaaS, AI marketplace, and HPC (high-performance computing), Solidus AI Tech is taking the Web3 industry by storm with its own 8,000-square-foot facility. 

What is the ChainGPT Incubation Program?

Providing support, guidance, and accelerating excellence, the ChainGPT Incubation program is an extensive, in-depth, multi-month-long program of intense, close collaboration between ChainGPT and promising projects that are looking to launch into Web3. 

Revealed in May of 2023, the incubation program aims to attract teams of innovators who have identified unique pain points of the digital economy; and equip them with the knowledge, tools, and connections to realize their visions.
