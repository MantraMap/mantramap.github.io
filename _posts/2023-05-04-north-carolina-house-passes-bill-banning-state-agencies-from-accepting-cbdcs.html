--- layout: post title: North Carolina House Passes Bill Banning State Agencies From Accepting CBDCs canonical_url: https://cryptonews.com/news/north-carolina-house-passes-bill-banning-state-agencies-from-accepting-cbdcs.htm tag: CryptoNews --- The North Carolina House of Representatives passed a bill that would ban state agencies from accepting payments in central bank digital currencies.
Lawmakers passed that bill 118-0 on Wednesday.  
The bill would encompass “any institution, bureau, board, commission, officer, or political subdivision of the State.” 
Republican lawmakers Harry Warren and Mark Brody sponsored the bill....
