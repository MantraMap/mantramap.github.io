European regulators have been quite steadfast in bringing regulations for the crypto industry. The swift introduction of the Markets in Crypto Assets Regulation (MiCAR) has been a testament to this. In the latest, the European Union’s banking watchdog has urged stablecoin issuers to voluntarily comply with consumer protection and risk management, in a year’s time.
