--- layout: post title: Bunzz Raises $4.5M Seed Round to Expand its Smart Contract Hub for DApp Development tag: CoinJournal ---

Singpore, Singapore, 24th January, 2023, Chainwire Bunzz a web3 development platform for decentralized applications, has completed a $4.5M seed round. Investors included gjmp, DG Daiwa Ventures, Coincheck, and Ceres. Funding will be used to help build out Bunzz’s Smart Contract Hub, which is already used by more than 8,000 dApp developers.
