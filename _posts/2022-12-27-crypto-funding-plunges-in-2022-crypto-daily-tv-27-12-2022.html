Crypto funding plunged in 2022.

VC investments in blockchain startups soared to a record $25.2 billion in 2021. In 2022, funding slowed dramatically and is projected to be about one-third of 2021’s record, following a wave of fallen companies, including hedge fund Three Arrows Capital, lender Celsius Network and exchange giant FTX.

Bitcoin hashrate drops nearly 40% as deadly U.S. storm unplugs miners.

The Bitcoin network hashrate has dropped by more than 38.8% from its peak on Wednesday, as many U.S.-based miners have been forced to switch down their facilities due to deadly blizzards.
