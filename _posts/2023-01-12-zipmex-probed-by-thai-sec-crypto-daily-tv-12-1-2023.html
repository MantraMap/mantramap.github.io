Bankrupt crypto lender Voyager's proposed deal to sell some of its assets to Binance US is a step closer to becoming reality. Thailand probes embattled crypto exchange Zipmex.

Thailand's securities regulator is probing whether embattled crypto exchange Zipmex, which is in the midst of being taken over, breached local rules in its offer of certain digital-asset products.

Group One discloses 13.5% stake in MicroStrategy.

Chicago-based Group One Trading disclosed in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing the ownership of nearly 1.3 million shares of MicroStrategy, which amounts to 13.5% of the company, or a dollar value of about $237 million at MSTR's current price of $188 a share.
