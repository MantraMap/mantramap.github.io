Bitcoin miner Argo Blockchain will avoid filing for bankruptcy protection, after it agreed to sell its Helios mining facility in Dickens Country, Texas, to Galaxy Digital for $65 million. The miner will also get a new $35 million loan from Mike Novogratz’s crypto-focused financial services firm.

Crypto exchange Kraken to stop operations in Japan.

U.S.-based crypto exchange Kraken said it would cease its operations in Japan next month, citing the current market conditions in the country and a weak crypto market globally.

The MBA Mortgage Applications released by the Mortgage Bankers Association presents various mortgage applications. It is considered as a leading indicator of the U.S Housing Market. The US MBA Mortgage Applications will be released at 12:00 GMT, the US Continuing Jobless Claims at 13:30 GMT, and the US Initial Jobless Claims at 13:30 GMT.

US Continuing Jobless Claims

The Counting Jobless Claims measures the number of individuals who are unemployed and are currently receiving unemployment benefits.

US Initial Jobless Claims

The Initial Jobless Claims measure the number of people filing first-time claims for state unemployment insurance.

US EIA Crude Oil Stocks Change

The EIA Crude Oil stockpiles report is a weekly measure of the change in the number of barrels in stock of crude oil and its derivates, released by the Energy Information Administration. The US EIA Crude Oil Stocks Change will be released at 16:00 GMT, Spain's Retail Sales at 08:00 GMT, and Italy's 10-y Bond Auction at 10:30 GMT.

ES Retail Sales

The Retail Sales measure the total receipts of retail stores. Monthly percent changes reflect the rate of change of such sales.

IT 10-y Bond Auction

The auction sets the average yield on the bonds auctioned off. Yields are set by bond market investors, and therefore they can be used to estimate investors' outlook on future interest rates.
