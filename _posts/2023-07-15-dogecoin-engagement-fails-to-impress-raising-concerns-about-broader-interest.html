Dogecoin New Addresses Stagnant The growth of new addresses joining the Dogecoin community has hit a roadblock since May, according to a recent DOGE price report. Social volume measures the number of mentions and discussions specifically related to a cryptocurrency across various platforms. In the case of Dogecoin, this metric indicates a significant decrease in overall online activity and conversations surrounding the coin.
