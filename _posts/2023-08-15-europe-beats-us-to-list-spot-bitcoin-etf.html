Jacobi initially received approval for this ETF in 2021 but delayed its listing due to crypto crashes in 2022 like the Terra collapse and the Three Arrows crisis. The Jacobi FT Wilshere Bitcoin ETF will trade under the ticker BCOIN and features a decarbonization plan.
