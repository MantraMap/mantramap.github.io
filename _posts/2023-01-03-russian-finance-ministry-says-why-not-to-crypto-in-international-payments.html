--- layout: post title: Russian Finance Ministry Says ‘Why Not?’ to Crypto in International Payments tag: CryptoNews --- The Russian Finance Minister has confirmed that his department wants to green-light the use of crypto as a payment tool in international trade. And he says that countries that “want to do business” with Moscow should be allowed to pay in crypto if it is “convenient” for them.
