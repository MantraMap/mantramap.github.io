On May 26, 2023, during the annual Cannes Film Festival, the World Investment Congress Awards ceremony took place. The winner of the Investment Persona Congress Awards was Ukrainian IT businessman Mykola Udianskyi. 

The award ceremony for the Investment Congress Awards 2023 was an unforgettable night dedicated to the best and brightest representatives of the investment industry. At the gala dinner, led by event organizer Anna Stukkert and Investment Congress President Thomas Miss, companies, funds, platforms, brokers, consultants, the fashion industry, PR and media outlets that made a positive contribution to the investment industry were recognized.

One of the most anticipated parts of the evening was the announcement of the Investment Persona Congress Awards. This year, the winner was Mykola Udianskyi, a renowned Ukrainian entrepreneur in the field of information technology and technology, a scientist, and philanthropist. Mr. Udianskyi was recognized for his contributions to blockchain and cryptography, as well as his charitable activities.

“The 'Person of the Year' award according to the Congress Awards 2023 goes to Mykola Udianskyi, who is a visionary Ukrainian technology entrepreneur,” commented Thomas Miss. "Mykola is the president of the EHOLD Group with 10 offices in 9 countries. EHOLD employs over 500 people serving a user base exceeding 10 million users in an impressive project portfolio.He is also the founder of the Qmall exchange, which is a Ukrainian market phenomenon. Despite such a difficult year for the country, the exchange was able to gather an active community of 400 thousand users.”

The organizers also acknowledged Mykola Udianskyi's charitable activities and his active civic position during the war with Russia.

"Mr. Udianskyi constantly helps Ukrainians who have lost their homes, income, and jobs during the war. Despite significant risks, he continues to invest funds in the restoration of his hometown, Kharkiv. He also supports Ukrainian soldiers and openly speaks about the war from internationally recognized platforms," said Anna Stukkert. "This is truly inspiring and deserving of respect and support from the global business community."

It should be noted that this is not the first award for Mykola Udianskyi. In 2022, he was recognized as the best digital currency expert at the World Influencers and Bloggers Awards and ranked first in Hacker Noon's 2019 list of the top 10 blockchain entrepreneurs in the CIS.
