Binance Australia on Thursday said it has officially ceased Australian dollar (AUD) deposits and withdrawals by bank transfer. The crypto exchange has also ceased all AUD trading pair. However, Binance users in Australia can still facilitate withdrawals and trading by converting AUD to USDT. Users can buy and sell crypto using credit or debit cards
