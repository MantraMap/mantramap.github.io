The world’s largest crypto exchange Binance on Tuesday announces enabling API functionality on Auto-Invest for all users. It allows users to automatically invest in their preferred cryptocurrencies amongst over 210 cryptocurrencies supported on Auto Invest. Auto-Invest is a dollar-cost averaging (DCA) strategy feature that allows users to automate crypto investments and grow their crypto holdings.
