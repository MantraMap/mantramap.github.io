A DAO-Oriented Model

DAO4ART, in addition to the above, as you can intuit, is a DAO-based production collaboration model. This decentralized autonomous organization allows artists to create and launch their own NFT collection on the platform through a collaborative process between the artists, curators, and investors.

The D4A DAO also includes a new and improved built-in royalty tokenization system, which allows artists to earn long-term income from their NFTs. This means that whenever their NFT is sold or traded on the platform, a percentage of the transaction value is automatically paid to the artist as a royalty.

The royalty tokenization system is implemented through smart contracts and self-executing programs that automate payment. This ensures that artists are fairly compensated for their work and can continue to earn income from their NFTs even after being sold.

However, what makes DAO4ART different from these royalties compared to the traditional model is that DAO4ART is tokenizing, implying that the royalty-embedded tokens will become a tradable asset class.
