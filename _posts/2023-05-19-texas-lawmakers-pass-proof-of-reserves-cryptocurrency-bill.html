--- layout: post title: Texas Lawmakers Pass Proof-of-Reserves Cryptocurrency Bill canonical_url: https://cryptonews.com/news/texas-lawmakers-pass-proof-of-reserves-cryptocurrency-bill.htm tag: CryptoNews --- The Texas legislature passed a bill this week requiring cryptocurrency exchanges to provide a “proof of reserves,” the latest move by the lone star state. 
HB-1666, co-sponsored by state Republicans Rep. Giovanni Capriglione and Sen. Tan Parker, applies to digital asset service providers that serve more than 500 customers or has $10 million in customer funds, and requires them to maintain reserves in an “amount sufficient to fulfill all  obligations to digital asset customers.”...
